Mahendra Singh Dhoni

A brilliant cricketing mind, captain cool, wicketkeeper, batsman, An awesome finisher

Here's a time line of MS Dhoni's life:

  • 1981 - Born in Ranchi, Bihar
  • 2001-03 - TTE at kharagpur Railway station
  • 2004 -A memorable Start to his ODI career.
  • 2007 - Winning the first ever World T20
  • 2009 - Leading India to the No.1 in Test Ranking
  • 2011 - Sealing India's first World cup in 28 years
  • 2013 - India's 4th double centurion as captain
  • 2013 - Taking India to the ICC treble after winning the all ICC major trophies.
  • 2020 - Retirement from International Cricket.
